Christian A Cappella At Berkeley

Founded by a group of Cal students in 2000, For Christ's Sake is a Christian a cappella group composed of students across campus fellowships and churches. We seek to inspire the Berkeley community to worship the Lord by glorifying Him and proclaiming the gospel through song and testimony.

FCS Auditions Are Now Open!

Interested in joining us this upcoming semester? Check out more about what it means to be a part of FCS by visiting our About page and find our audition details on our Auditions tab! We're looking for all vocal parts and vocal percussionists, and that might just mean you!

Our Performances

Concert

At the end of every semester, we put on a concert as the culmination of all of our hard work! Look out for updates on our next one as we approach May or December!

Sproul

If you can't wait until our concert to hear us sing, you can find us out in front of Sather Gate performing from 12:20 to 12:40 every Thursday!

Other Gigs

Besides weekly Sprouls and our semesterly concert, we've also sang at various events throughout our past, such as Project Light, Sing a New Song, and many others!
