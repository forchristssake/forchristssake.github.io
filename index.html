Christian A Cappella At Berkeley

Founded by a group of Cal students in 2000, For Christ's Sake is a Christian a cappella group composed of students across campus fellowships and churches. We seek to inspire the Berkeley community to worship the Lord by glorifying Him and proclaiming the gospel through song and testimony.
