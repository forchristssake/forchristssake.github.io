Audition For Us!

Who should audition for FCS?

Anyone who loves the Lord, loves to sing, and loves to praise and serve the Lord through music. And pray about it! FCS is a very time-consuming commitment, but it is also very rewarding, and God does so much in our group every single year! If that describes you, we'd love to have you audition for us!

Audition details

Audition Sign Ups: Sign up for auditions using the form below by Wed 1/27
Round 1: Submission Form opens 1/28, due Friday 1/29 at 11:59pm PST (audition details below)
Callbacks: 1/31 (email will be sent after midnight on 1/30 with details)
Results: Released 1/31 by midnight

Round 1 Audition Components

There are 3 parts to this audition:

  • Warm-Ups: Ascending and Descending
  • The assigned prepared piece: "The Lord Bless You": Please have your part (soprano, alto, tenor, bass) learned for the audition.
  • Solo Prepared Piece: Sing a short solo piece/excerpt of your choosing a cappella (without any music). You can sing anything from "Defying Gravity" from Wicked to "Oceans" by Hillsong to "Happy Birthday."
