Audition For Us!

Who should audition for FCS?

Anyone who loves the Lord, loves to sing, and loves to praise and serve the Lord through music. And pray about it! FCS is a very time-consuming commitment, but it is also very rewarding, and God does so much in our group every single year! If that describes you, we'd love to have you audition for us!

Right now, For Christ's Sake A Cappella is looking for all voice parts to audition for Spring 2023! It's geared up to be an exciting year, where we'll be attending Break it Down Philadelphia, a Christian a cappella conference hosted at the University of Pennsylvania, as well as inviting Christian a cappella groups from across California to join us for our very own Break it Down Berkeley!
