Audition For Us!

Who should audition for FCS?

Anyone who loves the Lord, loves to sing, and loves to praise and serve the Lord through music. And pray about it! FCS is a very time-consuming commitment, but it is also very rewarding, and God does so much in our group every single year! If that describes you, we'd love to have you audition for us!

Currently, For Christ's Sake is not looking for new members. Auditions typically happen at the beginning of each semester, so watch out for when that time comes around!

If you have any particular questions or concerns, you can contact us at our email, fcsberkeley@gmail.com.
